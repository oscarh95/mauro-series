The Joe Mauro Series

Created by Wolfboy8541

The Series

Joe Mauro is about a hard working guy that is trying to make ends meet. In school he is bullied and manipulated turning him into a rampaging monster which eventually leads to his death. In death Mauro begins to make new friends and develops into a better person.

Mauro and Friends

Along the way Mauro meets all types of people. Enemies to friends mostly and then back to enemies again. Keeping the peace isn't his strong suit.
