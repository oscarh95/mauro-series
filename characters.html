Meet The Characters

Joe Mauro

Our titular protagonist of the series; he faces many hardships in life without taking much compensation. Dreams of making it big and getting out of the hood.

Cena

One of the main antagonists of the series. Makes Mauro's life a living hell as well as showing him actual living hell.

Flax

An alien creature Mauro met in hell and vows to help him find the crystals of hell and save Mauro's soul.

Phil

The overseer of hell and a real chill guy. Follows Mauro closely to make sure he can face the challenges ahead. Motivates Mauro from time to time with tough love.

Dr. Levi

A scientist that had an unfortunate encounter with Mauro. He was missing in action for a while but is back and coming after Mauro.

Hottie

Hottie was an adversary of Mauro but after their encounter, Mauro befriended Hottie with his charisma and strength.

Johnny/Johnathan

Watches too much sc-fi movie and acquired mysterious powers. Proficient in sword to sword combat.

Elise

Joe Mauro's love interest and potential waifu.

Ent Man

One of the recruits in Joe Mauro's war effort against Lucy.

Michael Dodge

A loner hermit that just wants friendship. Has good intentions but his actions are misunderstood.

Lucy

Daughter of Phil and the successor to the throne.

Brian

Joe Mauro's bully in school.

Carl

Joe Mauro's best friend but they haven't seen each other yet.

Papa Cory

Cena's old boss before he got fired.

Mr. Handy

Cena's new leader who manipulated his mind into following him and obeying his orders.
